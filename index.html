BusTrackDC

An iOS app to find out when your bus is coming to a stop near you, using WMATA prediction data.

Download .zip Download .tar.gz View on GitHub

Do you live in or near Washington, DC and ride the bus? This app is for you!

Find the nearest bus stops, when the next bus is arriving, and where each route will take you, all in real time thanks to WMATA's data feeds.

I take the bus to work and I made this app after my favorite bus app stopped working earlier this year. I hope you find it as useful as I do!

The app is free, but if you think it's worth something, throw in a donation!
