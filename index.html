BusTrackDC

Do you live in Washington, DC and ride the bus?
BusTrackDC tells you when the next bus arrives!

Get it for FREE for your iPhone on the app store:

"BusTrackDC Adds Some Needed Competition to the D.C. Bus App Market" -- InTheCapital.com

"A new iPhone application being released today aims to fill the void left by the demise of the popular NextBus DC app..." -- DCist.com

"RT @j_ro: Hey DC folks, I just launched a free iPhone app to track when @wmata buses arrive. Check it out! j-ro.github.com/WheresTheBusDC/ #metroapi" -- @WMATA

Where's the nearest bus stop? When's my bus coming? Is that bus coming down the block going my way? Find out in real time thanks to WMATA's data feeds and BusTrackDC.

I take the bus to work and I made BusTrackDC after my favorite app stopped working. I hope you find it as useful as I do!
